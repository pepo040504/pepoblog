Hi, I'm Pepo

From a curious student exploring different paths to a cybersecurity enthusiast starting my journey at two of Europe's most prestigious tech universities. My story combines academic excellence with diverse passions - from calisthenics and DJing to investment strategies and continuous learning.

Currently beginning the joint EPFL-ETHZ Cybersecurity Master's program, ready to dive deep into the fascinating world of digital security while sharing every step of this adventure with you.

My Journey

2019

The Spark

My fascination with technology began when I first encountered programming during high school. What started as curiosity about how websites worked quickly became a passion for understanding the logic behind digital systems. I spent countless hours learning my first programming languages and building simple projects.

2020

University Beginnings

Started my bachelor's degree in Computer Science. The transition from high school to university was challenging, but it opened my eyes to the vast world of technology. I discovered my love for problem-solving and began exploring different areas within computer science to find my true calling.

2022

Discovering Cybersecurity

A cybersecurity course changed everything. Learning about network security, ethical hacking, and digital forensics felt like discovering a hidden world. I realized this field combined my love for technology with the excitement of solving puzzles and protecting others. This was my "aha!" moment.

2023

Deepening Expertise

Dedicated my final university years to cybersecurity projects and certifications. Participated in Capture The Flag competitions, built security tools, and gained hands-on experience with penetration testing. Each project reinforced my decision to specialize in this field.

2024

The Blog Journey

Started PepoBlog to document my experiences and help other students. Writing became a way to reflect on my learning journey and connect with like-minded individuals. The positive feedback from readers motivated me to continue sharing my insights and challenges.

2025

EPFL-ETHZ Adventure Begins

Got admitted to the prestigious joint Cybersecurity Master's program at EPFL and ETH Zurich! This marks the beginning of an exciting new chapter where I'll dive deeper into advanced cybersecurity concepts while documenting every step of this journey for my readers.
