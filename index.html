Why I Write This Blog

University can feel overwhelming, especially when you're navigating computer science studies and trying to break into cybersecurity. I remember feeling lost during my first years, wishing I had someone to share their real experiences and lessons learned.

That's why I started this blog. I write about the challenges I face, the mistakes I make, and the insights I discover along the way. Whether it's study techniques that actually work, navigating university bureaucracy, or diving deep into cybersecurity concepts, I share what I wish someone had told me.

My goal is simple: help other students feel less alone in their journey and maybe save them from some of the mistakes I made. If my experiences can help even one person feel more confident about their path, then this blog serves its purpose.
